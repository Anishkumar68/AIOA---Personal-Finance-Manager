✦ Based on analyzing your current implementation and the MVP plan, here are the essential missing features that should be in a complete personal finance app:

    🔴 Critical Missing Features

    1. Password Reset / Forgot Password
     - Currently no way to recover account access
     - Need: POST /auth/forgot-password, POST /auth/reset-password
     - Why critical: Users WILL forget passwords, no recovery = lost data

    2. Recurring Transactions
     - Rent, salary, subscriptions happen monthly
     - Need: Auto-create transactions on schedule
     - Why important: Saves time, ensures accurate tracking

    3. Bulk Transaction Import/Export
     - Export to CSV/Excel for tax records, backups
     - Import bank statements (CSV)
     - Need: GET /transactions/export, POST /transactions/import
     - Why important: Data portability, backups, analysis
     - pdf export also for upi apps transactions so we can multiple payment apps transactions in one place

    4. Transaction Tags/Labels
     - Categories are hierarchical, but tags add flexibility
     - Example: "#vacation", "#office" across multiple categories
     - Why important: Better filtering & insights

    ---

    🟡 Highly Recommended Features

    5. Goals / Savings Targets
     - "Save ₹50,000 for vacation"
     - Track progress towards financial goals
     - Why useful: Motivation, planning

    6. Multi-Currency Support
     - If you travel or work internationally
     - Currency conversion for accurate totals
     - Why useful: Real-world scenarios

    7. Attachments/Receipts
     - Upload photos of bills, receipts
     - Proof for taxes, disputes
     - Why useful: Documentation, audit trail

    8. Search & Advanced Filters
     - Date range presets (this week, last month, YTD)
     - Amount range filter
     - Multiple category filter
     - Why useful: Find transactions faster

    9. Transaction Duplication
     - One-click duplicate for recurring entries
     - Why useful: Quick data entry

    10. Bank Account Integration
     - Connect to bank APIs (Plaid, Yodlee)
     - Auto-import transactions
     - Why useful: Saves time, ensures accuracy
        - Note: This can be complex to implement, so consider for v2 if time-constrained

    10. Notifications/Alerts
     - Budget exceeded warning
     - Large transaction alerts
     - Bill due reminders
     - Why useful: Proactive money management

    ---

    🟢 Nice-to-Have Features

    11. Charts & Visual Analytics
     - Trend lines (income/expense over time)
     - Pie charts for spending breakdown
     - Why useful: Visual insights

    12. Calendar View
     - See transactions on a calendar
     - Why useful: Temporal patterns

    13. Split Transactions
     - One payment across multiple categories
     - Example: Amazon purchase = electronics + groceries
     - Why useful: Accurate categorization

    14. Debt Tracking (beyond loans i think we can add a flag in our Borrowed/Lent system)
     - Credit card balances, EMIs
     - Interest calculation
     - Why useful: Complete financial picture

    15. Export Reports as PDF
     - Monthly/yearly financial reports
     - Why useful: Sharing, records


    Future Enhancements:
     - 🔲 Recurring transactions (subscriptions, bills)
     - 🔲 File attachments for transactions
     - 🔲 User roles & permissions (admin, standard user)
     - 🔲 Integration with third-party financial APIs (Plaid, Yodlee)
     - 🔲 Webhooks for real-time updates
     - 🔲 Mobile app support (React Native, Flutter)
     - 🔲 autopay reminder with google calander or else
     - 🔲 AI-powered insights & recommendations
     - Password Reset - Security/access critical
